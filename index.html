our areas of experties

We understand that the tax audit system is quite energy-consuming, and there is a load of asymmetric information regarding the processes and materials that will be encountered in the audit. We have professionals who have years of experience in handling Tax Audits who can assist you in the audit process. The services we offer are from the issuance of the notification letter to the completion of the audit process, including assisting in meetings with tax officials and preparing the data needed in the audit as well as providing responses to the results of the audit.

Based on the tax rules, filing an objection to the Regional Office is one of the processes that must be followed, if the audit has not found a truth which according to the taxpayer should be revealed. Objection is an important process for presenting reasons and evidence that are not considered, which can be arguments to prove the truth. An objection is also a preparatory process and a first step in going through the appeal process. We have experience in assisting, preparing evidence and reasons based on tax rules as consideration in the objection process.

Filing an appeal to the tax court is an important process in tax disputes. Taxpayers are required to state the reasons for disapproval of the audit results and submit supporting evidence to the judge. This project covers the preparation of evidence, preparing the appeal letter and representing as tax lawyer in the appeal hearing. We have professional with tax and legal background to help you give a broad prospective and comprehensive reasoning in order to deliver arguments in the Tax Court.
